Clarry de`Vigri is a born and raised Central Oregonian with a wild country heart.
Her love for the freedom of wide-open plains and dense spooky forests is reflected in
her heroic, heart-centered, inspirational writing. Clarry has been creating stories
since she could talk and constantly dreams of building universes full of memorable characters.
As a devout Christian, Clarry aims to represent the faith in her books and knit the worlds of
fantasy and reality together as tightly as she can. The Academy of Purity and Blood is Clarry`s
first of many publications. She currently lives on a farm in Central Oregon with her family,
two funny dogs, noisy chickens, and lovely horses.
